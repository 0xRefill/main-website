WHAT IS $VLAD

A fair distribution rugproof reflect token on the BSC Chain. It auto yields rewards just for holding it. Every transaction incurs a 4% reflect fee (2%-holders , 2%-burn). $VLAD auto compounds in your wallet without doing anything. Hold and earn while others buy and sell to earn your part using our low reflect. The longer you hold the more you earn, working your way to immortality.

WHAT IS $aLIFE

aLIFE will be the token used for NFT minting on VLAD Finance's v2 platform. The majority of aLIFE will be emitted through our liquidity farming event. Each NFT minted will lock a portion of $aLIFE in the NFT to offer immediate liquidity when needed by its owner. Of the NFT sale, a majority is paid out to the original artist and a small portion goes to the treasury for VLAD Finance development funds. $VLAD stakers will also share a portion of profits from the sale.
